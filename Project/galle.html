Welcome to Galle





Le Grand Galle

Set in Galle, 300 metres from Galle International Cricket Stadium, Le Grand Galle has a number of amenities including an outdoor swimming pool, a fitness centre, a garden and free WiFi. The property is situated 600 metres from Dutch Church Galle, a 9-minute walk from Galle Fort and 1.1 km from Galle Light house. The property is 600 metres from Galle Fort National Museum.







Yara Galle Fort

In a prime location in the centre of Galle, Yara Galle Fort offers free WiFi throughout the property and a garden. This 5-star hotel offers luggage storage space. The property is allergy-free and is set 700 metres from Galle Fort Beach. hotel will provide guests with air-conditioned rooms offering a desk, a kettle, a fridge, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV and a private bathroom with a bidet. At Yara Galle Fort every room has bed linen and towels.



Blue Beach Galle

Set in Galle, 500 metres from Bonavista Beach, Blue Beach Galle offers accommodation with a fitness centre, free private parking, a terrace and a restaurant. This 4-star hotel offers a 24-hour front desk, luggage storage space and free WiFi. The property is allergy-free and is located 1.5 km from Jungle Beach.







Tabula Rasa Resort & Spa

Set in Galle, 700 metres from Koggala Beach, Tabula Rasa Resort & Spa offers accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, free private parking, a garden and a shared lounge. Located around 2.1 km from Talpe Beach, the hotel with free WiFi is also 13 km away from Galle International Cricket Stadium. The hotel has a terrace and sea views, and guests can enjoy a meal at the restaurant or a drink at the bar.



Odailem Resort - Talpe

Situated in Galle, Odailem Resort - Talpe is 7 km from Galle Light house and offers various facilities, such as a garden. Boasting room service, this property also provides guests with a restaurant. Guests can enjoy lake views. At the resort, all rooms have with pool view. Rooms come with a private bathroom with a shower and free toiletries. All guest rooms at Odailem Resort - Talpe are equipped with a seating area
