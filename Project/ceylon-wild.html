About

Ceylon Wild Safari-Yala offers an authentic and adventurous safari experience in the renowned Yala National Park, Sri Lanka. Situated amidst the untamed wilderness, the camp provides comfortable tented accommodations equipped with essential amenities, ensuring a cozy retreat in the heart of nature. Knowledgeable guides lead thrilling safari expeditions, offering the chance to encounter iconic wildlife, including leopards, elephants, and a rich variety of bird species. Guests can relax in the tranquil surroundings, savor delicious meals prepared with local flavors, and immerse themselves in the sights and sounds of the wild. With its focus on eco-friendly practices and genuine safari experiences, Ceylon Wild Safari-Yala promises an unforgettable adventure for nature enthusiasts and wildlife lovers alike.

For more details, visit Ceylon Wild Safari website
