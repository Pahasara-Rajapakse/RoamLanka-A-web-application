Welcome to Kandy





Cinnamon Citadel Kandy

Located 500 metres above sea level, Cinnamon Citadel Kandy offers a spectacular outdoor pool. It offers well-appointed air-conditioned rooms featuring spacious interiors with fine bedding and furnishing. Each room is equipped with a minibar, tea/coffee making facilities and a TV with cable channels.







Randholee Resort & Spa

Presenting beautiful views of Alagalla Mountains, Randholee Resort & Spa has an infinity pool, free Wi-Fi in public area and Ayurvedic massage. It enjoys a quiet location in Dumbara Valley, at the foot of Heeressagala Mountain. Randholee Resort & Spa is about 3.5 km from Kandy Railway Station and 20 minutes' drive from Kandy City Centre. Local attractions like Sri Dalada Maligawa Temple and Kandy Museum are within 4 km from the resort.



Amaya Hills Kandy

Located in Kandy, Amaya Hills, a leading 5-star hotel in Kandy, offers a hillside retreat overlooking the Heerassagala forest. Known as one of the best luxury hotels in Kandy, this hotel features traditional Sri Lankan architecture, an outdoor pool, a spa and a restaurant.







Nature Walk Resort

Approximately 10 minutes' walk from Kandy Lake, Nature Walk Resort offers accommodation set amidst tropical greenery. It features a restaurant and 24-hour front desk. Guests enjoy free Wi-Fi and free parking. Simply furnished, air-conditioned rooms are fitted with a dressing table. Shower facilities are provided in the en suite bathroom. Room service is also available upon request.



Eagle Regency Hotel

Situated in Kandy, 3.9 km from Ceylon Tea Museum, Eagle Regency Hotel features rooms with mountain views and free WiFi. All rooms boast a flat-screen TV with satellite channels and a private bathroom. The hotel provides river views and an outdoor pool.
